Execution Control

If this is a dynamic simulation, there will be a set of control buttons for running the simulation at the bottom of the view window. You will not have these controls in all applications. These buttons have the properties indicated below. "Step" means step one and only one time step of the simulation.

View Control

The view controls are as follows (some of these features may be disabled). To rotate, left-click and drag in the view window. To translate the view perpendicular to the camera view, right-click and drag in the view window. To zoom in and out, alt-left-click and drag in the view window or middle-button-click and drag.
